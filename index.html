May 5, 2021 — Russia
NASA `s Messenger
The rover is delivered to Mars.

The Mars 2020 mission with the Perseverance rover launched from Earth in July last year, and the rover successfully landed on Mars in February. The fifth rover in NASA history is equipped with seven scientific instruments to study the structure and geology of the Red Planet. The main task will be to collect samples of Martian soil, which are expected to be delivered to Earth in the future. On board Perseverance - an experimental device for obtaining oxygen from the atmosphere of Mars, as well as a "space helicopter", which is to fly for the first time on another planet.
